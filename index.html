Tourism

Batu city, located in East Java Province, about 100km South direction of Surabaya. Some people say that Batu is the Small Switzerland in Java because it is located at an altitude of 680-1200 meters above sea level with 15-19 degrees Celsius air temperature. There are dozens of tourist sites in this region which is also famous for its apple plantations. You can find the best tourism cave, waterfalls, campsite, and exotic journeys here.
